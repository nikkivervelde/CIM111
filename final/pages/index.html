Death of Nipsey Hustle

Nipsey Hustle was killed infront of his clothing store in Crenshaw. Nipsey was an iconic rapper while also having a prominent role in his community. He was a peacemaker between rival gangs blood and crips. He has been in the rap game since he was 16 years old. Yet he spent much of his time giving back to his community in Los Angelos.
